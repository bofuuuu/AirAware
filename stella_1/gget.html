Global Greenhouse Gas Emissions

This comprehensive map provides a visual representation of greenhouse gas emissions worldwide.
It highlights the varying levels of emissions across different regions, offering insights
into both natural and human-induced emissions. By pinpointing the areas with
the highest emissions, the map serves as a crucial tool for understanding and addressing climate change.
Whether used for research, policy-making, or public awareness, this map aims to facilitate
informed decisions and actions towards a sustainable future.
